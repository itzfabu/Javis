# Streamer HUD Widget - Premium Persona Tier Idea

## The idea

A paid upgrade tier for [[Streamer HUD Widget]]: different "Jarvis" personas/animations (alternate particle-sphere looks, behaviors, maybe voices) gated behind payment, on top of the free/base theme-and-size customization already shipped in `settings.html`.

## Why it matters / context

Base widget just reached v1 packaging (`StreamerHUD-v1.zip`) with free theme/size options. A persona tier is a natural upsell path once there's proof people actually buy/use the base widget - no point building it before then.

## Next step if pursued
- Revisit once the base widget has actual sales traction (units sold / active users), not before.
